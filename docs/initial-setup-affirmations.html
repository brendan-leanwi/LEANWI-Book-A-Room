LEANWI-Book-A-Room Affirmations Setup

If selected to be shown for a venue, the user is required to affirm that they have read and agree to certian conditions (or affirmations) before they can submit their booking. All affirmations will be shown when booking venues select to display affirmations.

Viewing Affirmations

  1. Go to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section.
  2. From this screen you may add, edit and delete affirmations.

Creating a New Affirmation

Steps:

  1. Go to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section and click the 'Add Affirmation' button in the top left.
  2. You can now type or paste your affirmation into the text area.
  3. Click 'Add Affirmation' to save.
  4. Once saved you can keep adding affirmations by entering the text into the text area and clicking 'Add Affirmation'.
  5. To view your saved affirmations navigate to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section.

Editing an Existing Affirmation

Steps:

  1. Go to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section and click the 'Edit' button of the affirmation you wish to edit.
  2. You can now edit your affirmation in the text area.
  3. Click 'Save Changes' to save the new affirmation.
  4. To view your edited affirmation navigate to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section.

Deleting an Existing Affirmation

Steps:

  1. Go to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section and click the 'Delete' button of the affirmation you wish to delete.
  2. Select 'OK' to confirm you want to delete the affirmation.
  3. To view your affirmations navigate to the LEANWI-Book-A-Room > Affirmations section.
